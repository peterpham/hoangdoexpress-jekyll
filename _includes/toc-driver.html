Introduction

Hoang Do Australia Pty Ltd trading as Hoang Do Express (“Hoang Do Express”) provides technology services (hereinafter referred to as “the Technology Services”) to independent drivers who provide transport services to Customers (as defined in the Definitions below) to and from airports and overseas passenger/boat terminals. The Technology Services provide a means to connect independent drivers with Customers, to enable independent drivers to provide transport services to Customers.
  2. The Technology Services enable independent drivers who execute this Agreement to:
    1. receive requests for transport services from Customers, and
    2. provide transport services to Customers (as defined in the Definitions).
  3. This Agreement (“the Agreement”) constitutes a legally binding agreement between you, the independent driver (“You”) and Hoang Do Express.

Fares and Commission
  1. Fare Calculation and Payment to You: You may charge a fare each time you complete a trip as part of the Transport Services you provide to a Customer via the Technology Services (“Fare”). The Fare will be determined once a Customer makes a booking, and will be calculated by taking into account the vehicle size, estimated distance (as determined by Hoang Do Express using location-based GPS mobile device) and estimated time for the trip (“Fare Calculation”). The Fare may fluctuate from time to time, depending upon the level of demand.
  2. You may also charge the Customer for any Tolls, airport surcharges, and other charges, taxes or fees incurred in your provision of Transport Services to Customers.
  3. You:
    1. appoint Hoang Do Express to be your payment collection agent solely so that the Fare, and applicable tolls, taxes and fees, can be paid by the Customer and received by Hoang Do Express on your behalf via a payment processing system; and
    2. agree that the payment made by the Customer to Hoang Do Express shall be considered the same as a payment made directly by the Customer to you.
  4. Hoang Do Express shall then pay to you on daily or weekly basis (after getting payment from the bank if customer paid by credit):
    1. the Fare less the Commission, as provided in clause 32 below; and
    2. any further amounts received to cover tolls, taxes, fees or other external charges.
  5. Changes to Fare Calculation: Hoang Do Express reserves the right to change the Fare Calculation at any time in its sole discretion, having regard to local market conditions or factors, and any changes in economic conditions.
  6. Changes to Fare: Hoang Do Express reserves the right to:
    1. withhold, reduce or adjust the Fare for a particular instance of Transport Services, for example if there is a technical error in the Technology Services, or if Hoang Do Express detects or finds any wrongful activity by you; or
    2. cancel the Fare for a particular instance of Transport Services if, for example, the Customer is charged for Transport Services that were not provided, or Hoang Do Express finds, as a result of a Customer complaint, that there has been fraud or other form of wrongdoing by you.
  7. Hoang Do Express shall exercise any decision to withhold, reduce, adjust or cancel the Fare in a reasonable manner.
  8. Commission: In consideration of Hoang Do Express providing the Technology Services to you, you shall pay Hoang Do Express a Commission on a per Transport Services transaction basis, which is equivalent to 15% of the Fare, excluding all taxes, tolls and other fees and charges.
  9. Hoang Do Express reserves the right to change the Commission at any time in its sole discretion, having regard to market conditions and factors, and business and economic conditions. Hoang Do Express will notify you of any changes to the Commission in writing. If Hoang Do Express exercises a discretion to increase the Commission, you may terminate the Agreement forthwith, without having to provide notice. However, if you continue to use the Technology Services and the Driver App after any such change in the Commission, you shall be deemed to have consented to the change.
  10. Cancellation Charges: You acknowledge and agree that Customers may cancel requests for Transport Services that you have accepted using the Driver App at any time prior to your arrival. If a Customer cancels a booking or request for Transport Services, or fails to cancel a booking then the following clauses will apply:
Cancellation of an OUTGOING Trip (a booking to Airports or Overseas Passenger/Boat Terminals):

Immediate pick up:

$10.00 will be charged to the Customer if the Customer fails to cancel the booking before a Driver arrives at the pick-up point.

EXCEPTION: if the Driver notifies the Customer (by telephone, text message or other form of electronic communication accordingly) that s/he cannot arrive within 15 minutes of acceptance of the booking (or will be unable to arrive at all) due to unforeseen circumstances, then the Customer is free to cancel the booking without further penalty or charge.

Pre-booking:

20% of the pre-determined Fare will be charged to the Customer if the Customer fails to cancel the Pre-Booking at least 1 hour before the agreed or pre-determined pick up time.

EXCEPTION: if the Driver fails to notify the Customer at least 45 minutes before the specified or pre-determined time (by telephone, text message or other form of electronic communication accordingly) that s/he will be unable to arrive at the pick-up location by the specified or pre-determined time (or will be unable to arrive at all) due to unforeseen circumstances, then the Customer is free to cancel the booking without further penalty or charge.

Cancellation of an INCOMING Trip (a booking from Airports or Overseas Passenger/Boat Terminals):

Immediate pick up:

$10.00 will be charged to the Customer if the Customer fails to cancel the booking before a Driver arrives at the pick-up point.

EXCEPTION: if the Driver notifies the Customer (by telephone, text message or other form of electronic communication accordingly) that s/he cannot arrive within 15 minutes of acceptance of the booking (or will be unable to arrive at all) due to unforeseen circumstances, then the Customer is free to cancel the booking without further penalty or charge.

Pre-booking:

20% of the pre-determined Fare will be charged to the Customer if the Customer fails to cancel the Pre-Booking within:

(a) 12 hours before the agreed or pre-determined pick-up time in relation to Customers arriving on incoming flights or boats from overseas; and

(b) 3 hours before the agreed or pre-determined pick-up time in relation to Customers arriving on incoming flights or boats within Australia.

IMPORTANT NOTE:

  • The Driver is obliged to wait a maximum of 1 hour after the pre-determined pick-up time for the Custom to arrive (“the Waiting Period”);
  • If the Customer fails to turn up or be available for collection and transportation by the Driver within the Waiting Period, the Customer will be charged an amount that equals 20% of the pre-determined Fare for the Incoming Trip.

EXCEPTION: if the Driver fails to notify the Customer at least 45 minutes before the specified or pre-determined time (by telephone, text message or other form of electronic communication accordingly) that s/he will be unable to arrive at the pick-up location by the specified or pre-determined time (or will be unable to arrive at all) due to unforeseen circumstances, then the Customer: (a) is free to cancel the booking without further penalty or charge, and (b) receives a credit that is equal to 20% of the pre-determined Fare for the Incoming Trip.

The preceding paragraphs are just a brief summary of the cancellation of a booking; thus, Hoang Do Express refers to, and strongly recommends that you carefully review this issue as set out in clause 4 “Charges and Fares” of the Terms and Conditions between Hoang Do Express and Customers, for the Fares and other charges which apply to the Customers of Transport Services, including Travel Now Bookings, Pre-Bookings, and cancellation fees or charges.
